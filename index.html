About Us

Moov is founded on a simple idea to offer a smart, green and sustainable alternative for short distance commute in urban cities.

We work in close collaboration with local governments, corporations and communities to cultivate the riding culture and improve urban living.

The team is made up of highly experienced and passionate individuals who are dedicated in contributing to the success of mobility transformation.

Solutions

Our smart mobility network consists of shared bicycles, scooters and E-bikes.
